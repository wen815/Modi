Narendra Modi

Born on 17th September 1950,in Vadnagar, Modi is an Indian politician working as the primary minister of Republic of India since 2014.

In 1978 Bachelor degree in political science from Delhi University.

In 1982 Master of Arts degree in political science from Gujarat University.

From 1960s to 1985-A member of National Volunteers.

In 1985-Joining Bharatiya Janata Party(BJP).

In 1988-Secretary-general of BJP in Gujarat.

Since 2014-Primary Minister of India
